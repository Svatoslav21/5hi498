About AdPriori

The idea is simple: you enter known or assumed parameters — budget, CPM, expected click-through and conversion rates, average order value — and the system calculates the rest.

Not only the obvious metrics, but the ones that actually determine whether your campaign succeeds or fails: maximum profitable CPC, break-even point, profit per ad dollar, and whether your unit economics can support scaling. You see results instantly, in real time, as you adjust any variable.

Reminder: outputs depend entirely on your inputs. Use conservative assumptions when planning budget.

FAQ

Does AdPriori guarantee profit?

No. AdPriori estimates outcomes based on the assumptions you enter. Real performance can differ due to auction dynamics, creative fatigue, attribution, tracking gaps, and market changes.

What inputs should I start with?

Start with conservative estimates for CPM, CTR, CVR, and AOV. If you have historical data from past campaigns, use medians (not best-case results).

I found a bug or wrong calculation — what should I send?

Please email us with: your iOS version, steps to reproduce, and screenshots of your inputs and results. Avoid including any sensitive information.

How do I request deletion of my data?

If you only use the App offline, your data stays on your device. For support emails, contact us and we can delete related correspondence.
